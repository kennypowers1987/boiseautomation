The Elephant in the Room: AI & Delivery

Yes, we use AI. We leverage Claude, Cursor, and offshore engineering talent to move fast and keep costs reasonable. But we don't outsource quality control. Everything is managed locally in Boise, and no code makes it to production without strict manual review and sign-off from our local engineering team. We don't hire additional agencies—we work with long-term, trusted individual contributors.

We are an engineering partner, not a staffing agency. We don't rent out our developers for staff augmentation or matchmake consultants for your internal teams. We operate as a unified, long-term team that is deeply invested in the quality of the final product we build for you.
